What is AxiumMC?

Embark on a unique and exciting adventure! AxiumMC is a community-driven Minecraft server meant for players of all ages and playing ability. We use a completely custom Kingdoms plugin that allows players to create their own settlemens and upgrade them into massive empires. New Bosses and Enchantments are only some of the custom features and mechanics available to everyone in our community. Our experienced staff team is active and ready to help with all of your needs. We would love to welcome you into our community!

Kingdoms

Kingdoms is our custom-coded variant of towny with many added features, including unlockable perks and a unique progression system for leveling up your settlement!

Bosses

In the Nexus Realm, you may encounter certain mobs that are unlike anything you've seen before. These are Mythic Mobs: curated to provide a unique and customized experience with many fun challenges and rewards.

Custom Enchants

Did someone say "custom enchantments"? Yep. Our custom enchantments are updated every week and add new mechanics to the game. Visit our wiki to learn more!

Brewery

Learn the art of brewing through fermenting, distilling and barrel aging to craft fine beverages offering experiences that have never existed in Minecraft before.

Caution: Please drink responsibly.

Jobs

Our carfeully curated jobs system provides a fun and convenient way to get paid for breaking, placing, killing, crafting, and more!

Community Events

At AxiumMC, we value building strong relationships with our members. One of the best ways to achieve this is by hosting community-wide events, minigames and online activities meant to bring our players closer together. If you have a good idea for an event, please feel free to send us your suggestions. You are also welcome to host your own event!
