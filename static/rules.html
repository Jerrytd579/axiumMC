Server Rules

Below are the official rules for AxiumMC. Please familiarize yourself with the rules under each category.
Failure to follow these rules will result in consequences according to the severity of the violation.

    1.1 Respect other players on the server.
    This includes: no harassing, slandering, name calling, targeting, and mini-modding.

    1.2 English only.
    We are an English speaking server. All messages in public channels must be in English.

    1.3 No discrimination.
    Discrimination of any kind is not permitted and will be met with a permanent ban without any chance of appeal.

    This includes:

    • a. Racism
    • b. Sexium
    • c. Homophobia
    • d. Xenophobia

    This list is not extensive. Anything else is up to the discretion of staff.

    1.4 Excessive caps, spamming, and special characters are not allowed.

    1.5 No public politics.
    Political conversations and debates are to stay out of public chat, on both the server and discord. Political conversations through private channels such as private messages, town chat, and party chat are explicitly allowed.

    Staff will not hold judgement towards players holding different political ideologies and will not respond to reports pertaining to political conversations held in the channels listed above.

    1.6 No public inappropriate content.
    Inappropriate or otherwise 18+ content is not permitted in public channels.

    1.7 Zero tolerance doxxing or DDoS policy.
    Any acts of Doxxing or DDoS threatening of such will be met with an immediate ban without appeal.

    Doxxing is defined as sharing others' personal information without their consent. Other people's information is only theirs to share and no one else's. There is no private information you can reveal of another player without their consent. None. This is punished heavily.

    1.8 Use our discord ticket system for major concerns or issues.
    This is the only place staff members will address issues including (but not limited to):

    • Punishment appeals
    • Player reports
    • Rule concerns
    • Donation issues

    2.1 Exploits and hacks are not allowed.
    Unintentional gameplay mechanics that are used to do something normally impossible, gain an unfair advantage, circumvent server rules, etc. are considered exploits.

    • Hacks are unapproved modifications to your client.
    • Exploits include using plugin features in ways unintended by the staff team.
    • If you think you found a serious exploit, contact a staff member or create a support ticket in our discord. Please DO NOT post major exploits in public channels.

    Approved modifications include:

    • Optifine
    • Shaders
    • Resource packs, excluding X-ray textures or other resources that give you an unfair advantage
    • Inventory Tweaks
    • Armor HUD mods that do not display other players' locations
    • Litematica (no print mode or easyplace)
    • Macros are limited to basic commands and crafting. If you have another use for macros, ask a staff member for approval.

    2.2 PvP and Towns.
    Do not toggle PvP within town limits without warning to those within the town. You must give players time to leave before toggling pvp.

    2.3 No teleport killing.
    Leveraging non-vanilla based teleporting is not permitted. This includes /tpa killing, /home killing, /t spawn killing, etc.

    3.1 Do not grief any town claimed areas.
    Do not grief within areas claimed by towns. If the block is claimed, do not grief. This also includes taking items from chests.

    Removing a building of an active player within your town without permission from that player is considered griefing

    3.2 Resource gathering must be done in the resource world.
    Excessive griefing of the towny world will not be tolerated. This includes 1x1 pillars, lava casing, and other random structures which do not belong in the Kingdoms world.

    3.1 One week notice.
    You must give town residents at least one week's notice before making adjustments to town rules, downgrading or removing their residency status, and unclaiming occupied plots of land.

    3.2 Town owner inactivity
    If the town owner is banned or otherwise removed, the senior most resident of the town will inherit leadership of the town.

    3.3 No new player exploitation
    You may not invite players to a town with the sole intention of making money off of them. This includes changing the joining fee before invite, changing taxes before taxes collection, etc.

    5.1 Bypassing the AFK timer is not allowed.
    Any methods that bypass AFK are considered punishable exploits.

    5.2 Excessive redstone/lag machines are not allowed.
    Redstone machines that are excessive or built for the purpose of creating lag are not tolerated. If you have any questions, please ask the staff team.

    5.3 Do not impersonate staff or mini-mod
    Do not impersonate a member of staff or mini-mod. Giving another player a "verbal warning" is considered mini-modding, while pointing out a given rule to another player is not.

    5.4 Do not attempt to bypass punishments.
    Bypassing a punishment such as a ban or mute by using an alt will result in your alt being permanently banned.

    5.5 No scamming
    Scamming will not be tolerated under any circumstances.
